How to Cook Yangnyeom Chicken

Sweet and Spicy Korean Fried Chicken

This is a simple and easy recipe for yangnyeom chicken(korean fried chicken). This post isn't by any means something I want people to try out, but something I've decided to post for the sake of learning the basics of HTML. On another note, I highly doubt that this recipe follows any traditional korean way to cook and is just the way I make yangnyeom chicken.

Ingredients

Yangnyeom Sauce

  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/3 cup cooking alcohol
  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup gochujang
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoon toasted white sesame seed

Fried Chicken

  • 500 grams Boneless chicken thigh
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 eggs
