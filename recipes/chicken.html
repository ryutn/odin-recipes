How to Cook Yangnyeom Chicken

Sweet and Spicy Korean Fried Chicken

This is a simple and easy recipe for yangnyeom chicken(korean fried chicken). This post isn't by any means something I want people to try out, but something I've decided to post for the sake of learning the basics of HTML. On another note, I highly doubt that this recipe follows any traditional korean way to cook and is just the way I make yangnyeom chicken.

Ingredients

Yangnyeom Sauce

  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 1/3 cup cooking alcohol
  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup gochujang
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoon toasted white sesame seed

Fried Chicken

  • 500 grams Boneless chicken thigh
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 eggs

Steps

Yangnyeom Sauce

  1. Mix everything except vegetable oil and sesame seeds into a bowl
  2. Once everything has mixed, add half of the vegetable oil and mix
  3. Let it rest until the chicken in cooked

Fried Chicken

  1. Cut the chicken thighs into one bite portions
  2. Season the portions with salt and pepper
  3. Mix eggs in a separate bowl
  4. Mix baking soda and flour in a separate bowl
  5. Dunk chicken thighs into the eggs
  6. Dunk chicken thighs into the mixture of flour and baking soda
  7. Fry chicken till golden brown
  8. Let the chicken rest for 10 minutes
  9. Refry in higher temperature to add more texture

Yangnyeom Chicken

  1. Spread remaining vegetable oil on a pan and let it heat up
  2. Stir fry the fried chicken with the yangnyeom sauce
  3. Add sesame seeds
  4. Serve
